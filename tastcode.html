Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and programs.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by clicking the "Enroll Now" button on the course page. You'll be guided through the payment process and gain immediate access to the course materials.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes! Our placement cell works with top companies to help our students find great opportunities. We provide resume reviews, mock interviews, and direct referrals to our hiring partners.

Can I access the course material after completion?

Absolutely! You get lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates. You can revisit the content anytime to refresh your knowledge.
